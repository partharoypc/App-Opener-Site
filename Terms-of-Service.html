Frequently Asked Questions

What is Views Grow?
Views Grow is a smart link generation service platform. We're just like bitly but with a smart twist.

Views Grow helps you by providing you your own smart link which lets you open a link directly in the app instead of opening links in the built-in browser.

For eg. If you are a YouTuber, whenver you share link of your YouTube channel in the Instagram's link-in-bio, it opens the link in the browser inside the Instagram app. But if you generate an Views Grow smark link of your YouTube channel and put that in your link-in-bio, it will redirect user to directly to the YouTube app which in turns gives you more engagement and great user experience.
How is it different from bitly?
Bitly is a URL shortner. You can just decrease the length of your links from Bitly. Whereas Views Grow not only shorten your URL but also customize and recreate a new smart link of the same. Which means you can share a customized and shorten link and it will redirect you to the native app where the URL belongs.
How much does it cost?
It's 100% FREE. You can avail this service for lifetime, from anywhere at anytime!
What kind of links can i generate from Views Grow?
You can generate any kind of link from Openin App which you want to open in the native app.
What is estimated revenue that I see on the dashboard?
Estimated revenue is an estimate of additional earnings from ads.

When you create a new smart link from Openin App and share it with others. Users are being redirected to the native app through openinapp link where they can see the ads which they originally can't when the link opens in-browser app.
What are the statitistics or insights that I can see for a link?
OpeninApp provides the following insights for every smart link you generate through it:
  • Total clicks on the link - How many users have clicked on the link
  • Per Day Click on the link (shown through a graph)
  • Estimated revenue from the link - The additional revenue users earned through ads after users are redirected to the native app
  • Top sources - Top sources from where the clicks are coming from. For eg. Facebook, Instagram, Twitter etc.
  • Top devices - Top devices from which the clicks are coming from. For eg. Mobile, Desktop, Tablet
  • Top locations - Locations from where the clicks came from
  • Age distribution - Age distribution of the audience who clicked on the link
  • Gender distribution - Gender distribution of the audience who clicked on the link To get more insights and analytics on other metrics, feel free to contact us and we'll get it done for you.
